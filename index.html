About Me

Wilson Calil is a senior marketing executive with more than 15 years’ experience with Fortune 500 companies in product marketing, business intelligence, consumer insight, branding and digital marketing. He’s headed up marketing teams, marketing campaigns and product development projects in Brazil, Hispanic Latin America, the Caribbean, the USA and China.

As an industry leader in marketing, Wilson developed and launched more than a hundred products and led dozens of consumer research studies. In 2015, Wilson was named Latin America’s Top Marketer by the New York-based digital magazine Portada-Online and was selected as one of the Top Fifty Latin American Marketers by The Internationalist magazine.

In his “spare time,” Wilson is currently working on a disruptive new digital platform to develop more effective ways of recruiting and hiring marketing professionals and agencies.
